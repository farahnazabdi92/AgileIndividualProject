Biography Summary

I am an IT professional with over five years of hands-on experience across software engineering, infrastructure, security, and data-driven systems. My journey in technology has been shaped by curiosity, continuous learning, and a strong desire to solve real-world problems in practical, meaningful ways.

I began my career during my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering, working as a software engineer while studying. In those early years, my focus was on software design, database development, and data-related work using SQL and SQL Server, alongside web technologies such as Java, JavaScript, and WordPress. This foundation trained me to think analytically and design systems that are both structured and scalable.

Driven to deepen my expertise, I pursued a Master’s degree in IT, which led me to one of the most challenging and rewarding roles of my career: Server Administrator and Cyber Security Specialist at one of the largest banks in my home country. Earning this role required dedication and persistence, and it allowed me to grow beyond software into enterprise infrastructure and security. I worked extensively with physical servers, F5 firewalls, web and application security, Active Directory, Microsoft Exchange, and large-scale system deployments-protecting critical banking systems and ensuring reliability at scale.

Alongside this role, I worked part-time as an IT Specialist in an industrial company, contributing to ERP system design, database development, website creation, and end-to-end IT support. This experience strengthened my ability to bridge technical solutions with real business needs.

As technology increasingly revolves around data and artificial intelligence, I chose to migrate and pursue a second Master’s degree in Artificial Intelligence. I am currently in my second year, focusing on Python, machine learning, natural language processing (NLP). I strongly believe that the most powerful solutions come from combining disciplines not choosing just one.

Today, I work as an IT Support Officer, where my programming background enables me to investigate deeply, identify root causes, and deliver effective solutions for clients. In parallel, I am a Python tutor at Girl Programming Network (GPN), teaching young girls how to code and empowering the next generation of women in technology.

I bring a unique combination of technical depth, adaptability, and a strong learning mindset turning complexity into clarity and challenges into solutions.
